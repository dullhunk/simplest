SO, WHAT SCHOOL DID YOU GO TO?

Have you ever been asked WHAT SCHOOL DID YOU GO TO? I was once asked this in a high-stakes job interview and my answer was that of a startled rabbit caught in the headlights before becoming roadkill.   • How is the school I attended relevant to my suitability for a given job?
  • Was this a friendly warm-up question, an innocent icebreaker or an inappropriately tricky tiebreaker?
  • Was the purpose of the interview to enable the Human Resources department to tick some box for #Equality, #Diversity & #Inclusion before abandonding me by the roadside as deliberate interview roadkill?
  • Maybe the interviewer would like to know where they could shove their stupid questions?
